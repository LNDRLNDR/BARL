What is a BARL Overlay for Rocket League?

A BARL Overlay is a free stream overlay for Rocket League that provides a game HUD that goes on top of your Rocket League. It contains a scoreboard with fully automated series score, team boost meters to enhance the viewer experience, and full design customization so you can fit it to your brand and events.
